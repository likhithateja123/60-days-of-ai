# Day 08 - Personal Environmental Health Analyzer

## Objective

Build an AI-powered environmental health dashboard using Claude.

## City Analyzed

Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India

## Features Generated

* AQI Analysis
* Environmental Health Score
* AQI Comparison Charts
* PM2.5 and PM10 Visualizations
* City Rankings
* Health Risk Indicators
* Personalized Recommendations

## Key Learnings

* AI can generate complete interactive dashboards.
* Environmental data can be visualized effectively.
* AQI and water quality directly affect health.
* Interactive dashboards improve data understanding.
