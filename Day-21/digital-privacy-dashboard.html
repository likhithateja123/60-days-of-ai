Methodology & limits. This tool runs entirely in your browser using a static reference dataset built from each company's publicly available privacy policy, App Store / Play Store "data safety" labels, and commonly reported industry practices as of early 2026. It does not connect to the internet, does not access any of your real accounts, and cannot see your actual activity, contacts, messages, or files. All scores (Digital Footprint Score, Privacy Score, heatmap intensities, radar values, and the Digital Twin profile) are modeled estimates, not measurements — they're designed to be directionally useful, not certified or audited figures. Parent-company mappings are inferred from public ownership records and may not reflect the latest corporate structure. For an authoritative view of what a company actually holds on you, request your data directly from that company (most offer a "Download Your Data" tool) or consult its current privacy policy.
