Do not enter real patient data. Clinical decisions must always be validated by licensed professionals following your facility's protocols.

Observation Status — Patient Notice Required

Patients placed under Observation are classified as outpatients for Medicare billing purposes even when they occupy an inpatient bed. This affects cost-sharing, SNF eligibility, and self-pay liability. The NOTICE Act (effective 2016) requires written and verbal notification within 36 hours. Ensure the Observation Notice workflow is initiated before this admission is confirmed.
