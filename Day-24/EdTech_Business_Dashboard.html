🛡️Moat Sources
📊

Data Moat

Every quiz response trains a better personalisation model. Gets smarter with scale — competitors can't replicate without our data.

👥

Community Moat

Peer cohorts create switching costs (friends, streaks, shared progress). Users don't just leave a product — they leave a community.

🏫

Ambassador Network

500+ campus reps = distributed trusted salesforce competitors can't replicate fast. Each ambassador owns a micro-community.

💬

WhatsApp Habit Loop

Daily task delivery builds muscle memory. Users check our messages like a teacher's assignment — not like an app they have to remember to open.

🎯

Outcome Data

First-to-market tracking the quiz → job pipeline. A verifiable outcome dataset is worth more than any content library to B2B partners.

⚠️Key Risks & Mitigations

Price sensitivity kills conversion

Students won't pay ₹199. Mitigation: Free tier + 7-day trial + UPI EMI + peer referral discount. A/B test ₹199 vs ₹299 in Week 2.

Low completion → Week 1 churn

Users sign up, get their report, and ghost. Mitigation: WhatsApp daily tasks (1/day), cohort accountability, streak rewards.

Undifferentiated — seen as an Unacademy clone

Mitigation: Zero video content. Own the 'first job/gig outcome' positioning — not 'courses'. Never compete on content library size.

WhatsApp API policy change

Meta could restrict business messaging overnight. Mitigation: Collect email on sign-up. Build Telegram group as fallback from Day 1.

Expert AMA quality inconsistency

One bad session breaks trust. Mitigation: Vet experts before public sessions. Record all AMAs. Mandatory rating form after each session.

CAC spikes if organic stalls

Mitigation: Zero paid ads until organic MRR >₹50K. Ambassador programme is the CAC hedge — ₹80 vs ₹150 for paid.
