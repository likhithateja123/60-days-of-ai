# Day 17 - Vehicle Cost Analysis Dashboard

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Vehicle Cost Analysis Dashboard to compare fuel types, maintenance costs, environmental impact, and vehicle operating expenses.

## Vehicle Information

* Vehicle: Hero Splendor Plus
* Fuel Type: Petrol
* Vehicle Age: 3 Years
* Monthly Distance: 1000 KM

## Dashboard Features

* Cost per KM Analysis
* Fuel Type Comparison
* CO₂ Emission Analysis

* E85 Economics & Break-even Analysis
* Environmental Impact Metrics
* Interactive Charts & KPI Cards

## Key Findings

* Different fuel types have varying operating costs and environmental impacts.
* Maintenance and fuel efficiency significantly affect long-term ownership costs.
* Data visualization makes vehicle cost analysis easier to understand.
