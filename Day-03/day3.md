# Day 03 - Role Based Prompting

## Question Used

How can a B.Tech student prepare for a career in AI?

## Response 1: No Role

The response provided general guidance on learning AI, programming, mathematics, projects, and internships.

## Response 2: Founder Persona

The response focused on opportunities, networking, personal branding, entrepreneurship, and creating value using AI.

## Response 3: Developer Persona

The response focused on technical skills, programming, AI frameworks, GitHub projects, practical implementation, and career growth as a developer.

## Comparison

The same question produced different responses depending on the assigned role.

* No Role → General advice
* Founder → Business and growth perspective
* Developer → Technical and implementation perspective

## Key Learning

Role-Based Prompting helps generate more targeted and relevant responses by assigning a specific perspective or expertise to the AI.
