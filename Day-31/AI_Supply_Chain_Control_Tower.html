How to Play

Objective

Goal
Resolve supply chain alerts before they expire to protect KPIs and maximize score.
Duration
3 minutes. Alerts escalate in frequency as time runs out.

Priority Colors

🔴 Critical
Severe KPI damage if missed. Resolve immediately.
🟡 High
Significant impact. Act within 20 seconds.
🟣 Medium
Moderate impact. Handle when critical/high are clear.
⚪ Low
Minor issue. Can be delayed but don't ignore.
