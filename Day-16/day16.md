# Day 16 - Stock Fundamental Research

## Objective

Analyze a listed company using AI-assisted fundamental research and understand key financial metrics used in stock analysis.

## Stock Analyzed

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

## Analysis Performed

* Company Overview
* Market Capitalization
* P/E Ratio Analysis
* Revenue & Profit Trends
* ROE and ROCE Evaluation
* Debt Analysis
* Promoter Holding Review
* Strengths and Risk Factors
* Fundamental Quality Assessment

## Key Insights

* TCS demonstrates strong business fundamentals and consistent financial performance.
* Profitability metrics such as ROE and ROCE provide insights into operational efficiency.
* Promoter holding and financial stability are important indicators of company quality.
* Fundamental analysis helps evaluate businesses beyond short-term stock price movements.

## Key Learnings

* Learned how financial metrics are used to assess company performance.
* Understood the importance of revenue growth, profitability, and debt management.
* Explored how AI can organize financial information into structured research reports.
* Gained exposure to stock research and business analysis concepts.
