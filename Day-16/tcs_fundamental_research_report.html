Company overview

TCS is India's largest IT services company and the second most valuable IT brand globally. It provides software development, consulting, business process outsourcing, AI/cloud services, and digital transformation solutions to Fortune 500 companies worldwide. Its top verticals are BFSI (32%), Consumer Business (15%), Life Sciences (10.5%), and Manufacturing (8.8%). In June 2025, TCS partnered with Anthropic to roll out Claude AI to 50,000 of its associates — signalling a strong AI-first pivot.
